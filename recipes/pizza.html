Pizza

Description

Pizza can come in many shapes and sizes, but the typical round-shaped pizza is the most popular. It is so versatile that you could make a pizza every day for the rest of your life and never repeat a meal. Toppings can include:

  • Peperonni
  • Salami
  • Cheese
  • Basil etc...

This unordered list could basically go on forever, and like the song that never ends, we could live in the glory of pizza for all eternity.

Recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 500g White Flour
  • 300g Water
  • 15g Sugar
  • 7g Salt
  • 2.5g Yeast

Sauce

  • 1 Can Crused Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix Water, sugar and yeast in a bowl and let stand for a few minutes.
  2. Mix in flour, salt and olive oil, mix into a mess.
  3. Let the mixture set for 5 minutes, mix for 2 minutes, and repeat.
  4. Place dough in an oiled bowl and Let it rise for 2 hours
  5. While waiting mix sauce by placing all ingredients in a bowl. Have it sit in the refrigerator to help flavours infuse the tomato sauce.
  6. Flatten dough into a circle and press into a thin circle shape from the inside out.
  7. Add sauce, toppings and cheese as you desire and bake for 10 minutes at 550 degrees
