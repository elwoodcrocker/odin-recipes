Description

Pizza can come in many shapes and sizes, but the typical round shaped pizza is the most popular. It is so versital that you could make a pizza everyday for the rest of your life and never repeat a meal. Toppings can include:

  • Peperonni
  • Salami
  • Cheese
  • Basil etc...

This unodered list could basically go on forever, and like the song that never ends, we could live in the glory of pizza for all eternity.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 500g White Flour
  • 300g Water
  • 15g Sugar
  • 7g Salt
  • 2.5g Yeast

Sauce

  • 1 Can Crused Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix Water, sugar and yeast in bowl and let stand for a few minutes.
  2. Mix in flour, salt and olive oil, mix into a mess.
  3. Let the mixture set for 5 minutes and mix for 2 minutes and repeat.
  4. Place dough in oiled bowl and Let it rise for 2 hours
  5. While waiting mix sauce by placing all ingredients in a bowl at once. Have it sit in the refrigerator to help flavors infuse the tomato sauce.
  6. Flatten dough into a circle and press in to a thin circle shape from the inside out.
  7. Add sauce, toppings and cheese as you desire and bake for 10 minutes at 550 degrees
