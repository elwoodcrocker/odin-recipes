Description

Goulash is a delicious pasta smothered a tomato based sauce. you can add ingredients to you liking such as:

  • Ground Meat
  • Peppers
  • Brocolli

Goulash can be enjoyed in just a few minutes of cooking time. It is a quick and easy meal that anyone can make

Ingredients

  • 1lb Macaroni
  • 2 cans Tomato Soup
  • 1 can of Diced Tomatos
  • 1/2lb Ground Meat
  • 1 small Onion
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Tbls olive Oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef until cooked thoroughly
  2. Cook pasta untill aldente, follow directions for desired type of pasta
  3. mix everything together and add salt to taste.
  4. Serve while hot with your desired side
