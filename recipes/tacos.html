Tacos

Description

Tacos are for Royalty. The rich and smooth meat sauce cooked with the perfect amount of spice will perfectly compliment an assortment of toppings. The possibilities are endless.

like pizza, tacos can be enjoyed in just a few minutes of cooking time. It is a quick and easy meal that will have you drooling during the whole process.

Wraps!!! whats not to love? They're like a little bundle of joy that you can hold in your hand. they come in so many shapes and sizes, color and flavor. Surely there's one for everyone.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 2 tbsp taco spice
  • Toppings of your choosing
  • Wraps or shells to hold it all together

Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef until cooked thoroughly
  2. Drain any excess fat off the meat
  3. Add taco spice with a little water to help distribute it evenly in the ground beef. boil off water
  4. Add meat and any desired toppings to your awesome wraps
  5. Fold and be bold. Eat with pleasure.
